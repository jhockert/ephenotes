## How to Provide Feedback

### Feedback Channels

**1. Email (Preferred)**
- Send to: beta@ephenotes.com
- Include: Device model, Android version, issue description
- Attach: Screenshots if applicable

**2. Play Console Feedback**
- Use "Send feedback" in Play Store app listing
- Automatically includes device info

**3. In-App Feedback (Coming Soon)**
- Settings > Send Feedback
- Will be added in future beta update

### What to Include in Feedback

**For Bugs:**
- What you were trying to do
- What happened instead
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Device model and Android version
- Screenshots or screen recordings if possible

**For Feature Requests:**
- What feature you'd like to see
- Why it would be useful
- How you envision it working

**For General Feedback:**
- What you like about the app
- What frustrates you
- What's confusing or unclear
- Suggestions for improvement

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Will my beta notes transfer to production?**
A: No, beta and production are separate. You'll need to recreate notes in production version.

**Q: Can I use this as my main note app during beta?**
A: Yes, but remember it's beta software. Keep important notes backed up elsewhere.

**Q: How do I report a crash?**
A: Email beta@ephenotes.com with details. Play Console may also capture crash reports.

**Q: Can I invite friends to beta?**
A: Yes! Share the opt-in link with anyone interested.

**Q: Will there be more beta updates?**
A: Yes, we'll release updates based on feedback, typically weekly.

**Q: What happens after beta ends?**
A: We'll launch to production and you can update to the final version.
