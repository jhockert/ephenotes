# ephenotes Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 4, 2026  
**Effective Date:** February 4, 2026

## Introduction

ephenotes ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with the ephenotes mobile application (the "App").

**Key Point:** ephenotes does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All your notes and information stay exclusively on your device.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

ephenotes is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do NOT collect:

- **Personal Information:** No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any identifying information
- **Usage Data:** No analytics, tracking, or usage statistics
- **Device Information:** No device IDs, advertising IDs, or hardware information
- **Location Data:** No location tracking or geolocation data
- **Note Content:** Your notes never leave your device
- **Behavioral Data:** No tracking of how you use the app
- **Diagnostic Data:** No crash reports or error logs sent to us
- **Cookies or Tracking Technologies:** None used

## How Your Data is Stored

### Local Storage Only
- All notes are stored exclusively on your device
- We use encrypted local database (Hive) for secure storage
- Data is encrypted at rest on your device
- No cloud synchronization or backup
- No data transmission over the internet

### Data Security
- **Encryption:** Notes are stored in encrypted format
- **Device Security:** Protected by your device's security features (lock screen, biometrics)
- **No Network Access:** App does not request or use internet permission
- **Isolated Storage:** App data is isolated from other apps per Android security model

## Permissions

ephenotes requests minimal permissions:

### Storage Permission (Android < 10)
- **Purpose:** To save your notes locally on your device
- **Scope:** Limited to app's private storage area
- **Usage:** Only for reading and writing note data
- **No Access To:** Your photos, videos, or other files

### No Other Permissions
- **No Internet:** App does not access the internet
- **No Location:** App does not access your location
- **No Camera/Microphone:** App does not access camera or microphone
- **No Contacts:** App does not access your contacts
- **No Phone:** App does not access phone features

## Data Sharing

**We do not share any data because we do not collect any data.**

- No third-party services or SDKs
- No analytics providers
- No advertising networks
- No cloud storage providers
- No data brokers
- No government agencies (unless legally required, but we have no data to share)

## Children's Privacy

ephenotes does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages because:

- No data collection of any kind
- No user accounts or registration
- No social features or communication
- No external links or advertisements
- No in-app purchases

Parents can confidently allow children to use ephenotes knowing that no personal information is collected or shared.
