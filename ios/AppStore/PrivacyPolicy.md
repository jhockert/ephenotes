# ephenotes Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 4, 2026  
**Last Updated:** February 4, 2026

## Overview

ephenotes is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## The Simple Truth: We Don't Collect Anything

**ephenotes is designed with privacy as the foundation.** We don't collect, store, transmit, or have access to any of your personal information or notes.

## What Information We DON'T Collect

- **Personal Information:** We don't collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personal identifiers
- **Note Content:** Your notes stay on your device - we never see or store them
- **Usage Analytics:** We don't track how you use the app
- **Device Information:** We don't collect device IDs, IP addresses, or location data
- **Crash Reports:** We don't collect crash logs or error reports
- **Advertising Data:** We don't use advertising networks or tracking pixels

## How Your Data is Stored

- **Local Storage Only:** All your notes are stored locally on your device using secure local database (Hive)
- **No Cloud Sync:** Your notes never leave your device
- **No Network Access:** The app works completely offline and doesn't require internet connectivity
- **Device Security:** Your notes are protected by your device's built-in security (lock screen, encryption)

## Data Sharing

**We don't share any data because we don't have any data to share.**

- No third-party services
- No analytics providers
- No advertising networks
- No cloud storage providers
- No external servers

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data:
- There's no data to access, modify, or delete from our servers
- Your notes are entirely under your control on your device
- You can delete the app at any time to remove all data

## Children's Privacy

ephenotes is safe for users of all ages because we don't collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices (which would require a fundamental change to the app), we will update this policy and notify users through the app store update notes.
