### App Store Description (4000 characters max)

**Capture thoughts instantly. Organize effortlessly. Stay focused.**

ephenotes is a privacy-first note-taking app designed for quick capture and effortless organization. Perfect for busy professionals, students, and anyone who needs to jot down ideas on the go.

**✨ Key Features:**

**Quick Capture**
• Instant note creation with floating action button
• 140-character limit keeps notes concise and focused
• Lightning-fast save times under 100ms

**Smart Organization**
• Priority levels (High, Medium, Low) for importance
• Color coding with 10 beautiful preset colors
• Pin important notes to keep them at the top
• Icon categories for visual organization

**Powerful Search**
• Real-time search as you type
• Find notes by content or category
• Pinned notes appear first in results

**Privacy First**
• All data stays on your device - no cloud sync
• No analytics, tracking, or data collection
• Works completely offline
• Your notes, your privacy

**Beautiful Design**
• Clean, intuitive Material Design interface
• Smooth 60 FPS animations
• Dark and light mode support
• Accessibility compliant (WCAG AA)

**Archive & Restore**
• Swipe to archive completed notes
• 3-second undo window for safety
• Easy restore from archive screen

**Perfect for:**
• Quick meeting notes and action items
• Shopping lists and reminders
• Creative ideas and inspiration
• Daily tasks and to-dos
• Study notes and key points

**Why Choose ephenotes?**
Unlike other note apps that overwhelm with features, ephenotes focuses on what matters: quick capture and smart organization. The 140-character limit encourages concise, actionable notes while the priority system ensures important items never get lost.

Built with Flutter for smooth performance across all iOS devices. Supports iPhone and iPad with optimized layouts for each screen size.

Download ephenotes today and transform how you capture and organize your thoughts!
